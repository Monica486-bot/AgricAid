Community Monitoring & Practical Data Collection

Through field workshops and cooperative monitoring, community members collect simple, actionable observations that help improve soil health and crop performance.

Soil Health:

Practical nutrient checks and organic matter guidance learned during workshops

Soil Moisture:

Field moisture checks and simple thresholds taught in training

Temperature:

Simple temperature checks to inform planting timing and crop care

Humidity:

Observation techniques to identify disease risk and microclimate effects

Soil pH:

Portable pH test methods and interpretation for better nutrient decisions

Community Records:

Collected observations and simple measurements are recorded and shared within the community dashboard for planning and learning.
